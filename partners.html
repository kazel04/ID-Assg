WHAT WE DO

Through the fusion of Natural Language Processing and metaverse technologies,
we are building the world's first AI-staffed mental health space
located within the digital world


In this space, personalised coaching and para-counselling programmes are provided, with more features
under development to help human counsellors & coaches
boost their client satisfaction

We have also been fortunate to
have the opportunity to work with amazing partners to accelerate our progress in fulfilling our vision
of a better mental healthcare system for youths

Do take a look at their webpages to learn more about their causes!
